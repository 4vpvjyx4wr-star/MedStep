MedStep Quiz Generator

Precision-Built for Gunners

📋 Instructions
1

Click the settings gear on the left of the copy prompt button to adjust your prompt. Then click Copy Master Prompt to copy it to your clipboard.

2

Click the Microsoft ↗ button to open Copilot. Paste the prompt alongside your lecture slides.

3

MAKE SURE that you click "Smart" on the bottom next to the plus button in Copilot, and change it to "Think Deeper." If you do not do this, the quiz will NOT generate correctly.

4

Once Copilot generates the quiz (the format it uses is called JSON), copy all of that text and paste it into the box below this one.

5

To access my premade quizzes, click on the quiz library button on the top left. There, you can filter by block, exam, week, and class, and build a custom quiz with as many questions as you want from each lecture.
